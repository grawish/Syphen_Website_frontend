Ge68/Ga68 Generator

What

68Ge/68Ga generator (half-life: 270 days) provides an excellent source for the positron-emitting radionuclide 68Ga. 68Ga is mainly used for radiolabelling peptides which is used chiefly in nuclear medicine oncology for diagnostic applications

Calibration Activities

10, 20, 30, 40,50 mCi

Shelf Life

Generator: 270 days from the date of production
Eluate: 68minutes after elution time

Storage

Generator: At room temperature below 25 0C, in lead shield (Do not freeze)
Eluate: At room temperature below 25 0C, protected from light in lead shield

Packaging

Generator set:

  • 1 unit 68Ge/68Ga radionuclide generator
  • Patient Information Leaflet.

Mo99/Tc99m Generator

What

MON.TEK 99Mo/99mTc generator contains fission Molibdenium-99 (99Mo) adsorbed by aluminum oxide (Al2O3) in a glass column. Technetium-99m (99mTc) formed by the decay of 99Mo, is a radioactive isotope having a half-life of 6.007 hours. After eluting by 0.9% NaCl solution, 99mTc Sodium Pertechnetate solution which is isotonic, colorless, clear, sterile, non-pyrogenic and suitable for I.V. injection is obtained. (99mTc) Sodium Pertechnetate solution can be administrated to the patients directly as a diagnostic agent or for labeling the kits.

Calibration Activities

5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45, 50 GBq (Given values are for 99Mo activity on calibration date)

Shelf Life

Generator: 21 days from the date of production
Eluate: 12 hours after elution time

Storage

Generator: At room temperature below 25 0C, in lead shield (Do not freeze)
Eluate: At room temperature below 25 0C, protected from light in lead shield

Packaging

Generator set:

  • 1 unit 99Mo/99mTc radionuclide generator
  • 5 vials of 10mL and 5 vials of 5 mL sterile, non-pyrogenic 0.9% NaCl solution in the box,
  • 2 x 5 units sterile, non-pyrogenic evacuated vials in the box,
  • 10 units elution label for vial and 10 units label for lead shield,
  • 10 units of swab with alcohol of 70% isopropyl,
  • Patient Information Leaflet.
