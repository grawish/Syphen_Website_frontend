Medi-MIBI 500 micrograms KIT

Content

0.5 mg [Tetrakis (1 isocyanide-2-methoxy-2-methypropyl-) copper(I)] tetrafluoroborate

Description

10 mL glass vial contains sterile, non-pyrogenic, white coloured, 0.5 mg lyophilized powder. It is not administrated to the patients directly. In nuclear medicine, it is used with technetium-99m via intravenous, intrathecal, inhalation and oral routes for diagnostic purposes. 99mTc-MIBI solution is used for as a diagnostic agentfor investigating cardiac diseases, breast imaging, parathyroid imaging

Shelf Life

Medi-MIBI KIT: 24 months
99mTc-MIBI solution: 8 hours

Storage

Medi-MIBI KIT: At 2-8 0C in refrigerator and protect from light
99mTc-MIBI solution: At room temperature below 25 0C, in lead shield, protected from light

Packaging

Type I glass vial with bromo-butyl rubber stopper and aluminium flip-off cap in a box (6 vials/box)



DTPA Kit for the Renal Imaging

Content

35 mg diethylene triaminepentaacetic acid calcium trisodium hydrate (DTPA-Ca)

Description

10 mL glass vial contains sterile, non-pyrogenic, white coloured, 35 mg lyophilized powder. It is not administrated to the patients directly. In nuclear medicine, it is used with technetium-99m via intravenous, intrathecal, inhalation and oral routes for diagnostic purposes. 99mTc-DTPA solution is used for as a diagnostic agent in neurology, pulmonary imaging (with a nebulizer) gastroenterology and nephrology.

Shelf life

MON.DTPA KIT: 24 months
99mTc-DTPA solution: 8 hours

Storage

MON.DTPA KIT: At 2-8 0C in refrigerator and protect from light
99mTc-DTPA solution: At room temperature below 25 0C, in lead shield, protected from light

Packaging

Type I glass vial with bromo-butyl rubber stopper and aluminium flip-off cap in a box (5 vials/box)



MDP Kit for the Bone Imaging

Content

10 mg methylenediphosphonic acid (MDP)

Description

It is used with technetium-99m via I.V. injection in nuclear medicine for diagnostic purposes. 99mTc-MDP solution is used as a diagnostic agent for investigating bone diseases.

99mTc-MDP solution: 8 hours

Storage

Packaging
